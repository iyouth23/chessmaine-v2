Ian Youth's Beginner's Chess Workbook

March 18, 2016

2015 Maine State Scholastic K-8 Champion Ian Youth authored A Beginner's Chess Workbook as part of his final project at Breakwater School in Portland. Ian has graciously offered the use of this instructional guide book to the ChessMaine commuinty. We bring you a downloadable version of the book and an image of Ian visiting Bobby Fischer's grave during a recent trip to Iceland.

Susan Polgar Foundation Chess Curriculum

April 7, 2008

Grandmaster Susan Polgar has created a chess curriculum to assist in the teaching and learning of chess to beginner and intermediate players. Thanks to GM Polgar we can make this very useful tool available to you.
