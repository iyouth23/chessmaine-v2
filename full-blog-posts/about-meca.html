About MECA

The Maine Chess Assocation

06.19.21 The Maine Chess Association (MECA) is a statewide, incorporated, non-profit 501(c)(3) organization that promotes various chess related activities in Maine such as United States Chess Federation rated tournaments, match play between local chess clubs and scholastic events. Donations are welcomed and are tax deductible.

Click here to view the MECA Constitution in Adobe Acrobat (PDF) format. If you do not have Adobe Acrobat Reader, you may download it here.

The Maine Assocaition of Chess Coaches runs the annual Maine Scholastic Team and Individual Championships.

Join the Maine Chess Assocaition!

MECA works hard to promote and encourage chess activity in Maine. You can support MECA and its activities by becoming a member for the low annual fee of $12 for adults and $6 for students. Your membership will directly benefit chess at all levels in Maine. We ask you to consider becoming a member of MECA and we thank you.

Life memberships are available for $240.

To purchase memberships or make a donation, please visit our store.

Membership Benefits

  • Your membership will support the official website of the Maine Chess Association (chessmaine.net).
  • MECA memberships are required to participate in all Maine Chess Player of the Year events.
  • MECA memberships support prizes in the Maine Team Chess Tournament.
  • MECA memberships support recently increased prizes in the Maine State Championship.
  • And of course, becoming a MECA member gives you a warm feeling that comes from supporting chess in Maine!
