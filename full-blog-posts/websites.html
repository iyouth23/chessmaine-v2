Chess Websites of Interest

After an exhaustive search of chess webpages, we've distilled out a number of sites we think you might be interested in. If your favorite chess site isn't included here, please let us know and we'll be happy to add it.

365Chess Website 3.5 million game online database.

American Chess Equipment Website Great for schools and clubs, tournament standard chess equipment with quantity discounts.

Bobby Fischer Website An unofficial site with information on the former World Champion from the U.S.

Boston Chess Club Website Lessons, tournaments, and simuls.

CertStaff Website Links on all aspects of learning chess.

Chess.com Website Free online chess.

ChessAthlete Website This site focuses on openings, strategy, and endgames and features an "article of the month reviewing grandmaster games.

ChessBase Website The source for international chess news. Fantastic coverage of all important chess events around the globe.

ChessBites Website 5 million+ searchable games updated weekly, annotated games, endgame studies, puzzles, opening analysis, and much more - all for free!

ChessCafe Website Articles, interviews, book reviews, and more. Be sure to check out the Archives.

ChessElo Website Elo rating in simple, chess puzzles, and links.

Chess Federation of Canada Website The hub of Canadian chess.

Chess KIDS Academy Website Chess instruction for kids (adults can learn a lot here too!).

ChessGames Website An online chess database and community.

Chessgammon Website High quality chess and backgammon equipment from the U.K.

ChessLab Website An excellent games database for games played since 1845.

ChessLive Website USCF's online playing site.

ChessMeeting Website A nation-wide site that helps you find a chess club or meeting in any state.

ChessSetsWorld Website Unique chess sets at guaranteed lowest prices.

Chess Quotes Website Intriguing chess-related quotes categorized by player and topic.

ChessWorld Website Online chess and a whole lot more.

Dan Heisman Website Website of the award-winning chess instructor Dan Heisman. If you want to improve, you must check out Dan's site!

Free Internet Chess Server Website A great place to play chess on the internet - and it's free.

GameKnot Website Correspondence chess and ten minute games via the internet. This site has a huge capacity, including the ability to view all games played by current and former members of GameKnot. Basic services are free, or you can purchase a premium annual membership starting at $39.

GetChessBooks Website A website offering chess books for beginners to advanced players.

GM Maxim Dlugy Website Homepage of GM Maxim Dlugy.

GM Susan Polgar's Blog Website National and international chess news with an emphasis on scholastic chess and girls and women in chess. This site is updated round the clock!

Homeroom Chess Website A downloadable, reproducible chess curriculum that requires no chess experience.

IChessU Website Interactive chess lessons, games, and more.

Internet Chess Club Website The best site to play chess on the internet; outstanding live coverage of international chess events.

Jeremy Silman Website IM Silman's site. Lots of good content including outstanding book reviews.

Kaoori Chess Company Website Traditional, modern, and unique chess sets and equipment. 10% discount for the ChessMaine community with the discount code "chessmaine10".

Marcel Duchamp Website Marcel Duchamp was an artist and chess player. This site is a collection of his works, his biography, and exclusive articles for the public.

Maritime Chess Centre Website Chess in the Canadian Maritimes.

Massachusetts Chess Association Website Regional events and crosstables. MACA publishes the award-winning magazine Chess Horizons.

Monopoly City Website Interesting chess sets.

Mon Roi Website Manufacturers of the Personal Chess Manager, a handheld electronic device for recording moves over the board. Approved by both USCF and FIDE and endorsed by Garry Kasparov and Susan Polgar. These items show up at international and local tournaments with increasing regularity.

New England Chess Tournament Clearinghouse Website Updated announcements of chess tournaments across New England.

New Hampshire Chess Association Website Our chess compatriots to the west.

RedHotPawn Website Online chess.

Rochester Chess Center Website An eclectic selection of chess equipment.

Stan's NetChess Website Correspondence chess on the internet.

Staunton Chess Sets Website History and information on Staunton chess sets.

The Chess Drum Website An interesting site focusing on the accomplishments of Black chess players or those of African ancestry.

The Chess Store Website Online retailer of chess sets and related accessories as well as backgammon sets.

The House of Staunton Website The finest chess sets and boards anywhere on God's green earth!

The Week in Chess Website Detailed international tournament coverage with crosstables and links.

United States Chess Federation Website The official site of the USCF. News, tournaments, ratings, and more - an essential site.

United States Chess Federation Chess in Education Committee Website The USCF Education Committee research page. Reams of research indicating the benefits of chess for children.

World Chess Federation Website FIDE news, national chess federation news, and the place to get a player's FIDE rating.
