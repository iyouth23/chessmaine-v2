The Craft Makers Gambit: An Exhibition of Chess

04.21.21 The Craft Maker's Gambit: An Exhibition of Chess is a chess-inspired art exhibit hosted by the Center for Maine Craft and Maine Craft Portland that will be on view from November 5 - December 31, 2021. Maine Crafts Association invites MCA craft artist members who have been approved through the MCA retail jury process in all mediums to create upon the theme of chess for this exhibition.

Thanks to Ilene Fishbein for contributing to this report.

Details on becoming a Maine Crafts Association member and submitting an application to The Craft Maker's Gambit: An Exhibition of Chess can be found HERE.
