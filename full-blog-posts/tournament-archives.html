Tournament Archives

The Maine Tournament Archive Project is an ambitious undertaking with the goal of creating an easily accessible, searchable, online database of crosstable and player information from every USCF-rated tournament played in Maine dating from 1991 to the present.

Our goal is to leave "no tournament unturned." Click the buttons below to explore an exhaustive list of tournaments held in Maine!
