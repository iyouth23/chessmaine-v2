George Cunningham Brilliancy Prize Awarded to Wyatt Hendrix for First Quarter of 2021

06.28.21 The Maine Chess Association congratulates Wyatt Hendrix on winning the first George Cunningham Brilliancy Prize of 2021. The prize is for his game against Aaron Spencer in the 2020 Maine State Championship.

Thanks to Mike Dudley for contributing to this report.

A note that the Brilliancy Prize decided to evaluate multiple games from before the first quarter of 2021, in order to give games played before the re-establishment of the prizes a chance to be seen. Wyatt wins a prize of $50 for his game, which is posted here.

The next George Cunningham Brilliancy Prize will be awarded to a member in good standing for games played in April, May and June of 2021. Submit your games to any member of the Maine Chess Association Policy Board for consideration!
